My Profile! (づ⏓∀⏓)づ

My name is Eiman John D. Espedido but you can call me Ejay!
I am 17 years old, living at J. Gonzales Street, Barangay 4, Tanauan City, Batangas.
Born in September 26, 2003, and studying at First Asia Institute of Technology and Humanities.
I have been a Competitive Badminton player for the past 4 years


  I was born as an only child in the lovely hands of my mother, Joy Mae S. Duyag, and my father, Joseph G. Espedido, in the Land of Two Holy Mosques commonly known as Saudi Arabia. (I am a Roman Catholic)

  I spent most of my childhood days indoors playing video games and learning how to use the computer, in the state of Jizan, Saudi Arabia.

  The first school I went to was Al- Maarefah International School and I had lots of memories in that school, simply because it was my childhood school and where I learned most of the things that I know to this day.   Being one of the only South East Asian student in the crowd was one of the weirdest feelings that I had while I was there. People mostly looked at me weird but sometimes other students can be rude and discriminate unintentionally.

  Other than school the only thing I had was my laptop, where I learned how to play games and keep myself busy even on busy days, we could say I was a bit addicted but when it came to studying, I did my best to balance out everything that I needed and to keep myself happy and satisfied in the process.

  When I moved here in the Philippines, it was a very weird feeling, cause I was the only super chubby and super quiet student that would only talk if he found someone to have the same awkward feeling around new people and that was when I was 14.

  Now that I am 17 years old. I have learned to do many things and discover many new skills that I have never even thought about having when I was a kid, and I am proud of each and every accomplishment I have had in the past 5 years. And I'm still looking forward to what life has in store for me and that is the reason why I am thankful for each moment I have in my life
